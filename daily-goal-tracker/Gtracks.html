Help & About

Gtracks is your personal daily goal tracker designed to help you stay productive and organized.


Features:

  • Today's Goals: Keep track of your daily tasks. Check them off as you complete them to see your progress bar fill up.
  • Calendar: Plan ahead from 2026 to 2036. Select any date to add or manage reminders for upcoming events.
  • Statistics: Visualize your goal completion rate. View progress for a specific Day, Week, or Month.
  • Settings: Customize your experience with themes and access help information.
